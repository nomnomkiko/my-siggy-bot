Nominations:

Radiants, Ritualists, and Rittys are now able to nominate one member of their choice per nomination cycle. 
Hierarchical nominations:
- Only Radiant Ritualist can nominate Ritualist
- Only Radiant Ritualist and Ritualist can nominate Ritty
- Radiant Ritualist, Ritualist, and Ritty can nominate Ritty Bitty

Each member can nominate one person per nomination cycle, and ur nomination already counts as one vote. Members can also vote for up to three candidates per month on the nominations leaderboard.

Some votes carry more weight. Radiant Ritualist votes count 5x and Ritualist votes count 2x, which helps ensure experienced members still have stronger influence in the process.

There are also a few rules to keep things fair. Self-nominations are not allowed, bots can't be nominated and campaigning orr asking people to nominate you is not permitted or you will get Dunce Role. Candidates also need to meet internal activity and contribution requirements before they can be considered.

Receiving nominations does not automatically grant someone the role, nominations simply show who the community believes deserves recognition, after which the Ritual team will review the candidates before making the final decision.
